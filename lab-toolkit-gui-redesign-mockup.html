Lab Toolkit GUI — proposed redesign

Persona dropdown · actions as a list · login-on-select with a fresh session · default-visible collapsible token sidebar · Simple↔Advanced detail. Mockup, not final styling.

1Persona = dropdown

One persona at a time, replacing the card row.

2Select → Okta login, fresh session

Each change triggers a login (embedded browser, isolated session) so no SSO bleed between personas.

3Actions = vertical list

Stacked one below the other; click runs it, result renders on the left.

4Token sidebar, default-visible

Persistent & collapsible. Shows the identity token, scopes, and the animated token journey.

5Simple ↔ Advanced toggle

Advanced = raw/decoded tokens, scopes, endpoints, correlation ids. Simple = plain "authorized / denied & why," no JWTs.

Shown here in Advanced view with an allow (Susan). A denial (e.g. Frank, or Alex→Desk) stops the journey red at the failing step and, in Simple view, reads simply "Okta denied this — not entitled." Live values come from real Okta tokens; this mockup uses sample data.
